Stakeholder Value Criteria
Patient Does it work? Will it help my symptoms? Is it easy to use? Will I be able to use it as part of my daily life? Is it safe? Can I afford it (or is it covered by my insurance or a government health care program)?
Clinician Is there rigorous evidence that it works? Will patients follow through with it? How does it fit into my clinical workflow? Will it help me manage this patient better? Can I easily access patient data or outcomes?
Payer Will it reduce overall health care costs? Will it improve patient health outcomes? What's the evidence? Will patients actually use it? Is it cost-effective compared to other treatments? Will it reduce hospitalizations or ER visits?
Regulator Is it safe? Is it effective? Has it been tested in rigorous clinical trials? Is the mechanism of action well-understood? Are there adequate safeguards for patient safety?
