Finance team establishes budget assumptions and prepares budget package

The finance team then prepares the budget package. This package will go out to the department heads who need to make budget requests for their departments, so it will include all the information they need in order to do that. For example, for items that might not be known yet, like health insurance cost increases, the finance team provides a specific figure for everyone to use; if everyone assumes the same figure, it will be easier to update the entire budget to a higher or lower amount once the final numbers are available.
