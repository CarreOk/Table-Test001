Physician

Traditional Health Care Tasks New Health Care Tasks

Nurse

  • Administers medications
  • Monitors patient vital signs.
  • Monitor remote vitals and respond to real-time alerts
  • Support patients and families in navigating home care details and logistics
  • Diagnoses and manages acute conditions, ordering lab tests, medical imaging, and medications.
  • Conducts daily in-person rounds visiting patients.
  • Conduct virtual rounds and make decisions via telehealth platforms
  • Adjust medication plans based on remote monitoring data (e.g., blood pressure trends, heart rate measures, etc.)

Pharmacist

  • Reviews medication orders and handles medication documentation.
  • Counsels patients when they pick up medications
  • Coordinate home delivery of time-sensitive medications
  • Communicate with the remote care team about drug-interactions flagged with decision support tools

Social Worker

  • Reviews and assesses patient mental, emotional, and social health.
  • Connects patients to community resources and support systems.
  • Support caregivers who are managing care at home
  • Identify patient social needs through a virtual environment analysis
